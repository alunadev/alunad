# 📖 CASE STUDY

## 1. Context

In 2023, LALIGA launched a global rebranding.

This triggered a full transformation of the Official App:
- From a **results app**
- To a **fan engagement platform**

👉 Goal: make the app the **center of the fan relationship** :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}  

---

## 2. Problem

### Product limitations
- Matchday-only usage  
- Low retention outside games  
- No personalization  
- Limited content  

### Business gap
- Low registration and profiling  
- Weak monetization  
- No ecosystem centrality  

---

## 3. Strategy

The product strategy was built around 3 pillars:

### 1. Personalization
- Team-centric experience  
- Dynamic home and notifications  

---

### 2. Context-aware experience
- App adapts to:
  - Off-season  
  - Pre-match  
  - Live  
  - Post-match  

👉 “Designing for moments, not screens”  

---

### 3. Content at scale (AI)

- Automated video generation  
- Vertical formats (stories, reels)  

👉 Aligning product with modern consumption habits  

---

## 4. Execution (Process)

### 🔍 User research
Defined 4 archetypes:
- Dispersed & dynamic

- Sports enthusiast

### 🧩 Product definition

### ⚙️ Key implementations

- Stories & Moments (AI-generated video)

### 👥 Growth

# ⚖️ 8. KEY TRADE-OFFS

## 1. Growth vs Quality

**Decision:**
Prioritize user quality (PRU) over raw acquisition volume  

**Trade-off:**
- ↓ Registered users  
- ↓ PRU absolute  
- ↑ Conversion rate  
- ↑ Engagement  

👉 Outcome:
Higher value user base, better monetization potential  

---

## 2. Content Scale vs Editorial Control

**Decision:**
Adopt AI-generated content (WSC) at scale  

**Trade-off:**
- ↓ Manual curation  
- ↓ editorial control  
- ↑ Massive content supply  
- ↑ real-time relevance  

👉 Outcome:
Enabled daily engagement loops and content scalability  

---

## 3. Simplicity vs Depth

**Decision:**
Balance casual formats (stories, quizzes) with advanced stats  

**Trade-off:**
- Too simple → low differentiation  
- Too complex → low adoption  

👉 Outcome:
Dual-layer experience:
- Casual → video & quizzes  
- Advanced → stats & data  

---

## 4. Monetization vs User Experience

**Decision:**
Maintain ad-based monetization  

**Trade-off:**
- ↑ revenue potential  
- ↓ UX quality (ads friction)  
- Low CTR (~0.36%)  

👉 Outcome:
Need to evolve toward:
- better targeting  
- native formats  
- data-driven monetization  

---

## 5. Ecosystem Integration vs Product Focus

**Decision:**
Position app as ecosystem hub  

**Trade-off:**
- ↑ strategic value  
- ↑ complexity  
- ↓ focus on core experience  

👉 Outcome:
App becomes gateway:
- Fantasy  
- OTT  
- Store  
- Loyalty  

---

## 6. Real-time vs Performance / Complexity

**Decision:**
Integrate live data + real-time content  

**Trade-off:**
- ↑ engagement  
- ↑ technical complexity  
- ↑ dependency on external feeds  

👉 Outcome:
Stronger matchday experience and differentiation  

---

# 🧠 9. Key Learnings

### 1. Content is the main engagement driver
Video + real-time content > static information  

---

### 2. Personalization increases user value
Higher PRU conversion = higher monetization potential  

---

### 3. Engagement ≠ growth
Improving retention does not guarantee acquisition  

---

### 4. Data gaps limit optimization
Lack of connection between:
- content  
- user behavior  
- business KPIs  

---

### 5. Clear product opportunities
- Scale quizzes  
- Improve push strategy  
- Optimize ads  
- Connect data → personalization → monetization
